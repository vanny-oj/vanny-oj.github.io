About Me

Bridging AI &
Human Creativity

I'm Patience Ojimaojo Akpa, an AI Content Strategist and Prompt Engineer on a mission to make artificial intelligence accessible, practical, and empowering — especially for women and creators across Africa.

Through my ebook "AI Made Easy: A Beginner's Guide to Writing Smart Prompts", I've guided hundreds of beginners through the world of AI without technical overwhelm — designed for students, small business owners, and content creators.

I'm proud to be an EmpowerHerAI alumni through TechWomen Nigeria, where I built a machine learning-powered Loan Approval Prediction System — proving that AI skills can solve real challenges across Africa.
